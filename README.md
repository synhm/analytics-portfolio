# analytics-portfolio
Quantitative analytics projects from UW Foster MSBA — pricing, causal inference, and consumer segmentation.
## About
This repository contains analytics projects completed during my 
MSBA at the University of Washington Foster School of Business. 
Each notebook applies quantitative methods to real business problems, 
with a focus on translating data into actionable decisions. 
Tools used include Python, Statsmodels, Scikit-learn, and Pandas.

## Projects
- **Price Elasticity & Competitive Response** — Own-price and 
cross-price elasticity modeling across a multi-brand market
- **Causal Impact of Search Advertising** — Difference-in-Differences 
analysis across 200 markets to measure ad effectiveness
- **Consumer Segmentation via PCA** — Principal Component Analysis 
to identify distinct customer segments and business implications
